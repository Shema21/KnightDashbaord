Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Knight Watcher ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit and use our website (www.knightwatcher.com). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about how we handle your data.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you sign up or interact with our services, we may collect your name, email address, and any other details you provide.
  • Trading Data: If you log your trades, upload screenshots, or share insights, this data is stored securely.
  • Technical Data: We may collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, and device details to improve our services.
  • Cookies & Analytics: We use cookies to enhance your experience and analyze website traffic using third-party analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our trading journal services.
  • Enhance website functionality and user experience.
  • Communicate updates, promotions, and changes to our services (only if you opt-in).
  • Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access to accounts.

Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or to comply with legal obligations.
  • When integrating with third-party tools (such as Clerk for authentication) to enhance security and accessibility.
  • If you choose to share a trade publicly, that trade data may be visible to other users.

We take security seriously. All sensitive data is encrypted and transmitted securely. We implement strict security measures to protect against unauthorized access or misuse.

Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your data. You can:

  • Request to view, edit, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt out of promotional emails at any time.
  • Adjust cookie settings through your browser.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@knightwatcher.com.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. If any significant changes occur, we will notify users via email or an announcement on our website.

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at support@knightwatcher.com.
